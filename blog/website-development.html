Website Development Services

Choose from our tailored web development packages designed to meet your needs, from basic websites to fully customized solutions. Whether you're looking for a simple online presence or a feature-rich platform, we have a package that's right for you.

Basic Package

Basic Web Design: Includes up to 5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, Blog) with a clean and straightforward layout. Mobile-optimized for all devices. Basic customization of colors and fonts.


Shared Hosting: 10 GB storage, 100 GB/month bandwidth, free domain for the first year, WordPress installation, and basic support via email.


Maintenance: Monthly updates for WordPress and essential plugins, and monthly backups.


Ideal For: Small businesses and individuals needing a simple, budget-friendly website solution.

Standard Package

Intermediate Web Design: Includes up to 10 pages with a professional look. Mobile-optimized design, advanced features like contact forms, social media integration, and interactive maps. Basic SEO optimization.


VPS Hosting: 30 GB storage, 300 GB/month bandwidth, free domain for the first year, advanced WordPress setup, and enhanced support via email and live chat.


Maintenance: Monthly updates for WordPress and plugins, weekly backups, and monthly security checks.


Ideal For: Medium-sized businesses seeking a professional website with additional features and better hosting.

Premium Package

Premium Web Design: Unlimited pages with fully customized design, mobile-optimized, includes e-commerce functionality, membership management, and API integrations. Advanced SEO optimization.


Managed WordPress Hosting: 50 GB storage, unlimited bandwidth, free domain, daily backups, strong security, and 24/7 support via email, chat, and phone.


Maintenance: Weekly updates for WordPress and plugins, daily backups, and monthly performance analysis.


Ideal For: Large businesses or projects requiring extensive customization, high performance, and top-notch support.
